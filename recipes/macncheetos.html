"Mac N Cheetos"

Ingredients

  • Frozen Mac N Cheetos

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 475 Degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Put Mac N Cheetos on Baking sheet and insert in oven
  3. Bake for 30 minutes, or until crispy
  4. Flip and bake for an additional 10 minutes
  5. Remove from oven and set to cool for 13 minutes
